Welcome to Is My Water Pure

Founded to help create an interactive flowchart based on studies from Nur-Ul-Idah, ismywaterpure.com aims to bring an increased understanding of whether a partifcular water is of purifying nature or not.

The interactive 'flowchart' is now live!
